About The Project

You play as a newcomer in a coastal town where every smile hides a secret. Build relationships, unlock intimate routes, and decide who to trust while uncovering a conspiracy beneath the surface.

This is the first game by Soft Peachie, focused on rich writing, attractive renders, and meaningful choice consequences.

Mysterious bartender with ties to the city's hidden elite.

Rin

Selene
